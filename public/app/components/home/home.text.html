AACInfo is a monthly newsletter of all things AAC (Augmentative & Alternative Communication) edited by the ACE Centre. Information is sourced from suppliers, user forums, twitter and email lists. If you have a news item that you think should be included please add it here. You can view some example newsletters in our archive.
