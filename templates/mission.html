Mission

The AAC industry is fast paced, worldwide and broad. It is impossible keeping up with what is going on. The aim of the site is to create a concise summary of all things going on in the world of AAC. The author is YOU. The editor is the ACE Centre. You can read more about us here.

What gets accepted?

  • Unique, new, product releases, updates developments
  • Research and development projects
  • Jobs

What doesn't get accepted?

  • Regular training/update events from suppliers
  • Product advertisements (we are considering sponsorship however)
  • Education/non-AAC resources (although if they are felt to be useful to the AAC world they may be accepted under 'Related fields..')

So who edits this?

Members of the Service Delivery Team from the ACE Centre
